TallyBar puts a live word, character, and line count for your clipboard right in the menu bar. Copy any text from anywhere — TallyBar shows the count instantly. No clicking, no opening apps, no counting by hand.

Perfect for writers, editors, students, social media managers, marketers, and anyone who works under a length limit: tweets, headlines, ad copy, essays, abstracts, meta descriptions, SMS, push notifications.

WHAT YOU GET
• Live count in the menu bar — updates automatically every time you copy something
• Pick what to display: characters, characters without spaces, words, or lines
• Shorten the menu bar label to a single letter (c / w / l) when space is tight
• Click the menu bar item for all four counts at once
• Launch at login — set it once, forget about it
• Tiny app, zero battery cost

ONE HUNDRED PERCENT PRIVATE
TallyBar reads your clipboard so it can count it. That's the entire app.
• Clipboard contents stay on your Mac. Always.
• No network code. No analytics. No tracking. No accounts.
• Open source: github.com/johnnybabcock/tallybar
• App Sandbox enabled
• Privacy manifest declares zero data collection

REQUIREMENTS
macOS 13 Ventura or later.
